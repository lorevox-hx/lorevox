⚙️ Settings & Privacy

        
These settings are optional and session-scoped only. Nothing here is saved between sessions unless noted.

      


      

        
🎤 Microphone

        

          Enable voice input so you can speak to Lori instead of typing. Your browser will ask permission the first time.
          Voice input is optional. You can always type instead.
        

        

          

            
Off

          

          

            
          

        

        
Audio is processed in your browser — nothing is recorded or stored.

      


      

        
📷 Camera (Optional)

        

          Enable the camera for affect-aware mode. Lori can read facial cues to adjust her interview style — slowing down during emotional moments or offering a gentler follow-up.
          Camera is completely optional. Lori works normally without it.
        

        

          

            
Off

          

          

            
          

        

        
Video is processed locally and never leaves your device.

      


      

        
📍 Location (Optional)

        

          Sharing your approximate city helps Lori give more contextual responses — for example, referencing your local time of day or region. Only city and region are used. Exact coordinates are never stored or sent.
          Location is completely optional. Lori works normally without it.
        

        

          

            
Not shared this session

          

          

            
            
          

        

        
Location is used only for this session and is not stored to disk.
