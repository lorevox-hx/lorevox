# Timeline Spine Architecture

## Purpose

The Timeline Spine is Lorevox 7.x's first-pass temporal structure layer.

It gives Lori enough chronological grounding to interview more intelligently without turning the beginning of the session into a rigid intake workflow. The spine starts with the narrator's date of birth and birthplace, then builds a rough path from birth to the present using age anchors, era segments, and major life transitions.

## Core Idea

Pass 1 builds vocabulary and temporal structure.
Pass 2A builds the timeline spine from DOB to the present.
Pass 2B returns for narrative depth, scene capture, and memoir-quality storytelling.

## Opening Sequence

1. "When were you born?"
2. "And where were you born?"
3. "Thank you. That helps me begin building a timeline of your life so I can guide the interview more naturally."
4. "Are you ready to begin, or do you have any questions for me first?"
